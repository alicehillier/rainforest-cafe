Rainforest Cafe

We might be in one of the world's busiest cities, but walk through our door and you'll be transported to a beautiful rainforest.

Our Food

Our food is natural, healthy and delicious! We only use the freshest produce, and source it locally where possible.

Our Environment

We've hand-picked the best natural furnishings, sourced the most beautiful tropical plants and put together the perfect palette of soothing natural colours to make sure all of our guests get the most out of their Rainforest Cafe experience!

Our Partners

We've teamed up with farmers near and far to so we can offer our guests only the freshest and most delicious food. We believe that our colleagues deserve fair pay for their products, so we only work with fairtrade accredited suppliers!

Our Planet

We love nature, so we want to protect it! We support sustainable farming and use eco-friendly packaging where we can.
